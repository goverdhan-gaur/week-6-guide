# BENCHMARK CONVERSION RATES

| Stage | Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|-------|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| **Ad to Click** | CTR | <1% | 1-2% | 2-3% | >3% |

- CTR: 2%+
